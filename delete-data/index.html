Delete Your Data (keep your account)

This page explains how you can delete specific data without deleting your LanApp account.

Delete in the app

  • Word lists: Open the app → Word Lists → select a list → Delete. This also removes the words linked to the list.
  • Game history: Open the app → Conjupong → History → select a session → Delete.
  • Lesson sessions / turns: Open the app → Lessons → select a session → Delete.
  • Notes (word_memos): Open the app → Notes → select a note → Delete.

Request by email (if you cannot access the app)

Contact: lanapp.support@gmail.com

Please include: your account email and what you want deleted (e.g., “delete my word list named ‘French A1’”).

What is deleted / kept

  • Deleted: The selected items and directly related data (e.g., deleting a word list removes its words and links).
  • Kept: Your account and any data you did not request to delete.

Retention & timeframe

Requested deletions are processed promptly. We do not retain copies, except minimal anonymized security logs which may be kept for up to 30 days.

If you want to delete your entire account and all associated data, see: Delete Account.
